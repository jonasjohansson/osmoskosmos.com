Osmos Kosmos

This artwork is aspirational research, a proof-of-concept incorporating natural elements in order to form embellishment, allure and couture through the development of homegrown biosynthetic textile. The textile is derived from the forced symbiosis of yeast and bacterial culture under controlled conditions, adding petals and plants.

My aim is to grow a breathing and living organism on the body to create an “Osmos” - A second layer of life, a membrane for exchange. If made from the same substance as the world the human body does not end with its skin, but instead expanding into Kosmos.

Exploring the Impact of a body loosing control over environment and being lost in the journey of discovery - A bodys place when nature takes over. Growing living organisms on the surface of our bodies to heal the wounded soul perhaps Serendipity will occur and we will living in beauty. Beauty is healing.
